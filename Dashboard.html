Hawkstone Park

  • Hellidon Lakes Golf Members can now play at our sister golf club; Hawkstone Park.
    Tee times must be booked through the pro-shop at Hellidon and each member can play up to 5 times per year. 5 day can play Monday to Friday and 7 day at any time. Fairway points members can play at any time but will obviously use up their points.
    Tee times can be booked up to a maximum of 7 days in advance.
    Joe Kingston
